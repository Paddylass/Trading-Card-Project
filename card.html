Irish Wolfhound

Image from https://i.pinimg.com

Categorised as the tallest dog breed, wolfhounds are also known as "gentle giants".

  • Latin Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 32 inches (86 cm)
  • Average Lifespan: 6-10 years
  • Temperament: Calm, gentle, intelligent

Despite its large size and hunting background, the wolfhound has a gentle nature. A loyal dog which would defend his family with his life. Colours range from grey to fawn.
